Banana Milkshake

Milkshake Cup + Bananas + Milk + Ice (Blended)

1. Grab a milkshake cup.

2. Head to the refrigerator and choose "fruits" then select "Bananas".

3. Once you have bananas, make sure to choose "Ice" and "Milk" before continuing.

4. Once you have all of the listed ingredients, go to the blender and blend the bananas, milk and ice.
